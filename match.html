West Indies remained unbeaten in the group-stage. West Indies put a total of 218/5 which is the highest score in this World Cup. Even though Omarzai succeeded by taking Brandon King but he failed to stop Nicholas Pooran. He conceeded 36 runs in an over and hosts dominated the powerplay by scoring 92 runs losing 1 wicket.

After powerplay, Pooran slowed his innings but the cameos from Hope and Powell managed West Indies to keep runrate 10 per over. In the death overs, Pooran dominated the game with his over. He scored 24 runs in the Rashid Khan over and taken the score over 200. Pooran was just short of two runs for his 100. Omarzai's stunning throw from the deep cover broken the heart of Nicky P.

Afghanistan continued struggled with bat as they lost Gurbaz in the first over of powerplay. Ibrahim showed some classy shots but failed to score big.Mccoy removed Ibrahim,Nazibullah in the first over of his spell. After his wicket, Afghanistan batting falled very cheaply. Hosein,Mccoy,Motie continued to take wickets at regular intervals. In Afghanistan batting lineup only two batters crossed 20 run mark. West Indies shattered Afghanistan for 114 runs and they sealed the game by 104 runs.
